# English Test - Grade 5

## Topic: Past Simple (regular verbs): questions and short answers

**Instructions:** Complete the blanks.

---

## Part 1: Read and complete the questions

Read Mia's diary and complete the questions.

**Mia's diary (Saturday):**  
I **finished** my homework at 10 o'clock and **walked** to the park with my friend Anna.  
We **played** basketball and **looked** at some books in a small shop.  
I **arrived** home at 1 p.m. My grandparents **visited** us.  
They **asked** me about school and we **talked** for an hour.  
In the evening, Dad **emailed** my aunt and we **watched** a film.

1. What time ______ Mia ______ her homework?  
   At 10 o'clock.

2. Where ______ she ______ with Anna?  
   To the park.

3. What ______ they ______ in the park?  
   Basketball.

4. What ______ they ______ at in the shop?  
   Some books.

5. What time ______ Mia ______ home?  
   At 1 p.m.

6. Who ______ her grandparents ______ ?  
   Us.

7. What ______ they ______ Mia about?  
   School.

8. What ______ Mia's dad ______ in the evening?  
   My aunt.
